How to Play

Guess the word in 6 attempts.

  • Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word.
  • The color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.
  • The word changes every day.

Features unique to Wordle Ultimate:

  • Word Editing: Click on a letter box (or select with arrow keys) to change or remove its contents.
  • Practice Mode: Upon completion of the daily word, continue playing with randomized practice words.
